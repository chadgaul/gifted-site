FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the best way to keep track of Christmas gifts?
A dedicated gift tracking app beats spreadsheets and scattered notes because everything lives in one place: who you're shopping for, what you've bought, what it cost, and what still needs wrapping. Gifted organizes gifts by person and event and tracks each one from idea to given. Learn more →
How much should I spend on Christmas gifts per person?
A common guideline is 1–1.5% of your annual income for total holiday gifting, divided by relationship - more for immediate family, less for coworkers and acquaintances. Gifted lets you set an overall event budget plus individual per-person budgets with live progress bars. Learn more →
How do I remember gift ideas throughout the year?
Capture ideas the moment they happen. When someone mentions something they'd love, add it to their profile in Gifted with a photo, link, and price. By December you have a ready-made list for every person. Learn more →
How do I avoid buying duplicate gifts?
Duplicates happen when purchases aren't logged anywhere. In Gifted every gift has a status - idea, decided, purchased, wrapped, given - so a quick check before buying shows what's already covered, including gifts stashed away months ago. Learn more →
Is there an app to remind me about birthdays and gifts?
Yes - Gifted pairs date reminders with the actual gift plan. Create a birthday event with a countdown, get notified in time to shop, and see saved gift ideas and budget on the same screen. Learn more →
Can I use Gifted as a Christmas shopping list?
Yes. Every undecided or unpurchased gift becomes your to-buy list, organized by person with prices, store links, and budget remaining. Learn more →
Is Gifted free?
Yes - Gifted is free to download and use. An optional Gifted Pro subscription unlocks extras, but the core gift lists, budgets, and tracking are free. Get it on the App Store →
Is my gift list private and synced across devices?
Yes. Gifted is privacy-first: no account required, your data stays in your private iCloud and syncs automatically across iPhone and iPad. Nobody can peek at their presents.
